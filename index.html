Presentation of the project:


Hey there! Welcome to our interactive website that display some data visualizations on global life expectancy.

This interactive website allows you to analyze and compare the life expectancy of different countries from the years 2000 to 2015. We obtained the data for this study from Kaggle and the World Health Organization (WHO). The project is also open-source and available on our GitHub page.

The analysis is grouped into three main categories: mortality, diseases, and economic factors. By exploring these different areas, such as healthcare systems, disease prevalence, and economic situations, you will have a deeper understanding of the complicated factors that influence life expectancy.

We've worked hard to make this website as user-friendly and interactive as possible, so you can simply navigate through the data and discover insights for yourself. Happy exploring!
