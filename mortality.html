Correlated Factors Affecting Life Expectancy

This chart displays a correlation matrix of various factors that impact life expectancy. It helps to identify which factors are positively and negatively correlated with life expectancy and how strong the correlations are.

It is calculated as the covariance between two variables divided by the product of their standard deviations, hence if all the values in a column are the same, the correlation between that column and any other column is undefined.
